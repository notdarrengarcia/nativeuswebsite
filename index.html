Photography and Filmmaking

Capturing Moments

That

Last a Lifetime.

Hey there! My name is Darren Garcia and I am both a cinematographer and photographer. I picked up
a camera about 10 years ago and I haven't been able to put it down since. Over my career
I've managed to become a Gold Telly Award winner in Cinematography, winning alongside HBO for their
work in Game of Thrones. I am also an International Filmmaker having my films been submitted around the world.
My photogaphy has also shown up on many feeds on Instagram for companies like Topo Designs, Stetson, Type Watches,
among. At the end of the day, I'm just a guy with a camera who has a passion for capturing moments
that will last a lifetime.
